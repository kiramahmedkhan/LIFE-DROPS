About Us

LIFE DROPS is a nationwide voluntary blood donation organization founded with a single mission: to save lives by connecting blood donors with those in need, free of cost. We operate across all districts in Bangladesh through our dedicated network of volunteers, donors, and coordinators.

Our Vision

We envision a future where no one dies due to lack of blood. We strive to build a strong network of selfless individuals committed to humanity.

Our Mission

To ensure timely and safe blood availability to anyone in need, irrespective of location or background. We actively conduct blood donation campaigns, awareness drives, and training programs for volunteers.
